About WDJ WebApps

Welcome to WDJ WebApps – a collection of interactive and user-friendly web applications designed to make your daily life easier and more productive. Created by WKDJ Anuradha, these apps serve a wide range of purposes, from daily motivational quotes to astrology predictions and salary calculators.

Each app is custom-built to provide a smooth user experience, with a focus on functionality and modern design. Explore the collection and find tools that can help you stay motivated, make informed decisions, or even calculate your salary accurately.

Our Web Apps
